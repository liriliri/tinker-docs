# tinker-photo-collage

A photo collage plugin for [TINKER](https://github.com/liriliri/tinker), allowing you to arrange multiple photos into various grid layouts.

![Screenshot](/photo_collage.png)

## Features

- **Grid layout templates** — choose from a variety of layout templates for 2 to 16 photos
- **Drag and drop** — drag photos from the sidebar into layout slots
- **Customizable canvas** — configure canvas width, height, and background color
- **Padding, spacing, and radius** — adjust padding, spacing between slots, and corner radius
- **Photo adjustment** — scale and reposition photos within each slot
- **Grid resizing** — drag grid dividers to resize rows and columns
- **Background image** — set a custom background image for the canvas
- **Randomize** — shuffle photo positions across slots
- **Save and copy** — save the collage as an image or copy it to clipboard

## Usage

1. Click the **Photos** area in the sidebar to add photos
2. Select the number of photos and choose a **layout template**
3. Drag photos from the sidebar into the layout slots on the canvas
4. Adjust **padding**, **spacing**, and **radius** using the sidebar controls
5. Drag grid dividers to resize rows and columns as needed
6. Scroll within a slot to zoom, or drag to reposition a photo
7. Click **Random** to shuffle photo positions
8. Click **Save** to export the collage as an image, or **Copy Image** to copy to clipboard

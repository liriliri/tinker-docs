# tinker-music-player

A local music player plugin for [TINKER](https://github.com/liriliri/tinker), supporting playlist management and multiple audio formats.

![Screenshot](/music_player.png)

## Features

- **Multi-format support** — play MP3, FLAC, WAV, OGG, M4A, AAC, and WMA audio files
- **Playlist management** — create custom playlists, add/remove tracks, and manage a favorites list
- **Play modes** — sequential, single loop, and shuffle playback modes
- **Play queue** — manage a separate play queue independent of playlists
- **Lyrics display** — automatic LRC lyrics loading and synchronized display
- **Music detail view** — view album art and lyrics in a dedicated detail panel
- **Recent tracks** — automatically records recently played tracks (up to 100)
- **File search** — search and add local audio files directly from the player
- **Mini mode** — compact player window for minimal screen usage
- **Float lyrics** — display lyrics in a floating window on top of other apps
- **Volume control** — adjustable volume with persistent settings

## Usage

### Adding Music

1. Click the **Add** button in the toolbar to select audio files from your computer
2. Alternatively, use the search bar to find audio files on your system and add them directly
3. Tracks are automatically imported with metadata (title, artist, album, cover art)

### Playing Music

1. Double-click a track in the list to start playback
2. Use the player bar at the bottom to control playback (play/pause, previous, next)
3. Click the progress bar to seek to a specific position
4. Click the play mode icon to cycle between sequential, loop, and shuffle modes

### Managing Playlists

1. Use the sidebar to switch between **Local**, **Recent**, **Favorites**, and custom playlists
2. Right-click a track to add it to favorites or a custom playlist
3. Click the **+** button in the sidebar to create a new playlist

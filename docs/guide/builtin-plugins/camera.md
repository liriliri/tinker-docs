# tinker-camera

A camera plugin for [TINKER](https://github.com/liriliri/tinker) that accesses your device camera to preview the video feed and capture photos.

![Screenshot](/camera.png)

## Features

- **Live preview** — real-time video feed from your device camera
- **Photo capture** — take photos and save them as PNG files
- **Mirror display** — video is horizontally flipped for a natural mirror-like experience
- **Bilingual UI** — supports English and Chinese

## Usage

1. Grant camera access when prompted
2. The live video feed will be displayed on screen
3. Click the capture button to take a photo
4. The photo will be automatically downloaded as a PNG file

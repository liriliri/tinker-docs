# tinker-timer

A stopwatch and countdown timer plugin for [TINKER](https://github.com/liriliri/tinker), featuring a flip clock display style.

![Screenshot](/timer.png)

## Features

- **Stopwatch** — a precise stopwatch with minutes, seconds, and centiseconds display
- **Lap timing** — record lap times while the stopwatch is running, with split and total time for each lap
- **Fastest/slowest laps** — automatically highlights the fastest and slowest laps when there are two or more
- **Countdown timer** — set a countdown with hours, minutes, and seconds
- **Audio alert** — plays a beep sound when the countdown reaches zero
- **Start/pause/reset** — full playback controls for both stopwatch and countdown
- **State persistence** — countdown duration and active tab are saved and restored automatically

## Usage

1. Choose between **Stopwatch** and **Countdown** tabs
2. **Stopwatch**: click **Start** to begin timing, **Lap** to record a lap, **Pause** to pause, and **Reset** to clear
3. **Countdown**: set the desired hours, minutes, and seconds, then click **Start** to begin the countdown
4. The countdown will play a sound alert when it reaches zero

# tinker-video-player

A video player plugin for [TINKER](https://github.com/liriliri/tinker), supporting common video formats with playback controls, playlist history, and media information.

![Screenshot](/video_player.png)

## Features

- **Video playback** — play MP4, WebM, OGG, MOV, AVI, MKV, and M4V video files
- **Drag and drop** — drop a video file directly onto the player to open it
- **Playback controls** — play, pause, replay, seek forward/backward, and adjust volume
- **Playback speed** — toggle playback rate for faster or slower viewing
- **Picture-in-picture** — watch videos in a floating mini player window
- **Fullscreen mode** — enter fullscreen for an immersive viewing experience
- **Playlist history** — automatically records play history with thumbnails and duration
- **Resume playback** — remembers playback progress and resumes from where you left off
- **Media info** — view detailed file information including codec, resolution, FPS, bitrate, and audio sample rate
- **Caption support** — enable or disable captions when available

## Usage

1. Click **Open Video** or drag and drop a video file onto the player
2. Use the playback controls to play, pause, seek, and adjust volume
3. Click the **speed** button to change playback rate
4. Click the **picture-in-picture** button to float the video or the **fullscreen** button for full-screen mode
5. Click **File Info** to view detailed media information
6. Open the **Playlist** panel to browse play history and quickly switch between videos
